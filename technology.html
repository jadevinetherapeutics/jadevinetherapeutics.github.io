Rational ML-Guided Design

We utilize state-of-the-art generative and discriminative modeling techniques to design novel therapeutic agents.

By modelling the structure of the drug-target interaction, we enable rational ML-guided design of therapeutic agents. Our search can explore a vast chemical space and identify promising candidates with optimal properties. Our methodologies allow us to predict the structure and function of proteins with high accuracy, facilitating the development of targeted therapies.

At Jadevine Therapeutics, we find the intersection of our target discovery capabilities and our ML capabilities to be a key driver of innovation. Our integrated approach combines the power of modern machine learning tools to accelerate the discovery of new therapeutic targets and the development of effective treatments.
